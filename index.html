Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainability and environmental awareness on campus. We aim to create a greener and more eco-friendly environment for students, staff, and the community. Through education, advocacy, and action, we strive to reduce our carbon footprint and make a positive impact on the planet.

Recycle your waste and reduce environmental impact.

Save energy by turning off lights when not in use.

Plant trees and support biodiversity.
