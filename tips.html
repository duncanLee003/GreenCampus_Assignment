Reducing Waste

Here are some ways to reduce waste:

1. Reusable Alternatives: Switch to reusable versions of items like water bottles, shopping bags, and food containers to cut down on single-use plastics.

2. Shop Smartly: Buy in bulk to reduce packaging waste, and choose products with minimal packaging

3. Compost food scraps and yard waste to reduce landfill waste and create nutrient-rich soil.

Saving Energy

1. Turn off lights and electronics when not in use.

2. Use energy-efficient appliances

3. Unplug chargers and devices

4. Use natural light during the day instead of artificial lighting

5. Set your thermostat to an energy-efficient temperature when you are away from home

Greener Travel Options

1. Use public transportation or carpool when possible.
